Study Goal Tracker – Student Productivity System
📌 Project Overview

Study Goal Tracker is a web-based application designed to help students plan, manage, and track their study goals effectively.
It provides a centralized platform where students can organize tasks, monitor progress, and improve overall productivity.

In today’s fast-paced academic environment, students often struggle with time management, goal clarity, and consistency.
This project aims to address these issues through a simple and structured digital solution.

🎯 Problem Statement

Many students face difficulties in managing their study schedules, tracking academic goals, and staying consistent due to distractions and lack of proper planning tools.
Traditional methods such as notebooks or scattered applications do not provide centralized tracking, analytics, or structured goal management.

The Study Goal Tracker system is developed to solve this problem by offering a unified platform for study planning and progress monitoring.

✅ Objectives

To provide a centralized system for managing study goals

To help students track daily and weekly tasks

To improve time management and productivity
